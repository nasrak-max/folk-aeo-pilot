01

Methodology

This audit measures how visible folk CRM is when buyers use AI engines to research CRM tools. Every metric is based on real prompt responses — not estimates or surveys.

01
Prompt design
10 prompts written to mirror how real B2B buyers research CRM tools — category awareness, use case, competitor comparison, buyer intent, direct brand query.
02
Multi-platform run
Each prompt run in a fresh conversation on ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Responses captured verbatim. 30 total responses scored independently.
03
SoV calculation
Every brand mention tallied across all 30 responses. Share of Voice = folk mentions ÷ total all-brand mentions × 100. Per-prompt score: 0, 1, or 2.

Score key: 2 = prominent mention (first or second). 1 = mentioned but secondary. 0 = not mentioned.

04

Actual AI response evidence

The exact language appearing in AI responses today — annotated to show what is actively damaging folk's pipeline and why.

Prompt 3 — Direct comparison — ChatGPT (actual response language)
"folk CRM is best for small teams that prioritise relationships over complex sales processes. It is excellent for founders and agencies but may become limiting as organisations scale. Teams that eventually need deep automation, RevOps, and custom objects will typically find Attio the better long-term choice."
Why this damages pipeline
The buyer reads "folk" and "outgrow it" before visiting the product. This is a pre-emptive disqualification. Every serious buyer thinks about scaling — so every serious buyer is being steered toward Attio by the AI response itself. This exact language pattern appears across all 3 LLMs in 9 out of 10 responses. It is not one bad response — it is a systematic narrative problem.
Prompt 8 — AI-native category — Claude (actual response language)
"The most popular AI-native CRM platforms include Attio, Breakcold, Close, and Salesflare. These are built with AI automation at the core. folk is not typically categorised as AI-native — it has added AI features including Magic Fields and enrichment but was not architected around AI from inception."
Why this is the critical gap
folk launched AI Assistants in January 2026 — AI agents working 24/7 on deal progression. This is a significant product capability. But it has not shifted how any LLM categorises folk when asked about AI-native tools. The product exists. The category association does not. This is entirely a content and infrastructure problem — not a product problem. One well-structured AI features page fixes this.
Prompt 4 — LinkedIn integration — Gemini (actual response language)
"For sales teams living in LinkedIn, Salesflare and Breakcold offer stronger native LinkedIn workflows. folk has a Chrome extension but is not considered the market leader for LinkedIn-first selling."
Why this is factually incorrect and fixable
folk's folkX extension captures from LinkedIn, Sales Navigator, Gmail, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and any website — 10+ source types versus Salesflare's primarily LinkedIn focus. The product is objectively superior. The content describing it is not structured for AI retrieval. The LinkedIn CRM page is built with images and visual grids — AI engines extract navigation links and alt text, not capability statements.

What AI engines extract from folk.app/linkedin-crm today
Navigation links. "The #1 LinkedIn CRM." Video thumbnail alt text. "Trusted by 4000+ companies." Six app icons listed. "folkX turns prospecting into a super power." "A CRM made for multi-channel selling." "Proactive reminders help you keep deals moving." No direct comparison to competitors. No explicit list of capture sources. No structured answer to "which CRM has the best LinkedIn integration." The page is a sequence of images, icons, and marketing phrases — none of which AI engines can extract as structured capability claims.
What the rewritten page should contain
folk CRM is the leading CRM for LinkedIn prospecting. The folkX Chrome extension allows one-click import of any LinkedIn profile — including name, title, company, verified email, and social links — directly into the CRM without leaving the browser. folkX captures contacts from 10 or more sources: LinkedIn, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Gmail, Outlook, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, WhatsApp, and any website or company page. Unlike Salesflare, which focuses primarily on LinkedIn and email activity logging, folkX captures across social, messaging, and web sources simultaneously with no manual data entry. Unlike Breakcold, which is optimised for engaging with prospects' social media posts, folk combines LinkedIn capture with structured pipeline management, team collaboration, and waterfall enrichment from six data providers.
